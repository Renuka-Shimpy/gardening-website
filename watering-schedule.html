Watering Tips

💧 Check Soil Moisture

Always check the soil before watering. Stick your finger 1-2 inches into the soil - if it's dry, it's time to water.

🌅 Morning Watering

Water your plants in the morning to reduce evaporation and prevent fungal diseases.

🚰 Water Deeply

Water thoroughly until it runs out the drainage holes, then let the soil dry out before watering again.
