Our Story

Growing with passion since 2010

Meet Sarah Green

Founder & Head Gardener

With over 15 years of experience in horticulture, Sarah founded GreenBloom Nursery with a simple mission: to make gardening accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Her passion for plants began in her grandmother's garden and has grown into a lifelong dedication to sustainable gardening practices.

LinkedIn Instagram Twitter

🌿 Our Mission

To inspire and enable people to connect with nature through gardening, providing quality plants, expert guidance, and sustainable solutions.

👁️ Our Vision

A world where every home has a garden, and every garden tells a story of growth, care, and environmental stewardship.

💚 Our Values

Sustainability, Quality, Education, and Community - these principles guide everything we do at GreenBloom.
